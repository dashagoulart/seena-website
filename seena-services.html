Ozone Therapy

What is ozone therapy?

A medical treatment using controlled medical ozone (O₃) — a form of oxygen — to improve oxygen delivery, reduce inflammation, and support the immune system. Applied intravenously, rectally, intramuscularly, or locally depending on the doctor's recommendation.
