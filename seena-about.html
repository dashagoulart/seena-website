About Seena

Healing is not just
the absence of symptoms

Seena was built on a simple belief — that lasting health comes from understanding the whole person, not just treating what hurts.

Our story

Where Western science meets Eastern wisdom

Seena Integrative Health Centre was founded in Lisbon with a clear purpose: to create a space where medicine and natural therapies work together — not alongside each other, but as a unified plan for each patient.

We saw too many people moving between specialists who didn't communicate, receiving treatments that addressed symptoms but not causes. Seena was built to be the answer to that fragmentation.

Today our team includes medical doctors, kinesiologists, naturopaths, breathing coaches, massage therapists, and mind-body practitioners — all working from the same patient picture, with the same goal: long-term wellbeing, not short-term relief.

Our method

Science meets tradition

Every treatment plan at Seena follows the same structured framework — regardless of which service you come in for.

1
Assess with precision
Medical-grade diagnostics and functional assessments — before any treatment begins, we understand the full picture.
2
Integrate disciplines
Your care plan may combine medical and therapeutic services — acupuncture with naturopathy, massage with kinesiology. One plan, not isolated sessions.
3
Track and adapt
Regular check-ins, progress reviews, and plan adjustments. We stay with you as your health evolves — the relationship doesn't end after one visit.
"

We treat people, not symptoms — and we build every plan around the full picture, not just the reason someone walked in.
